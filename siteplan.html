Site Plan Document

Site Name

Jonam Plumbing Materials

This name represents a company that supplies modern plumbing materials and equipment, including taps, pipes, and fittings. "Jonam" is distinct and professional, suggesting a reliable business identity. The site will help build brand awareness for customers looking for high-quality plumbing materials.

Optional domain availability: jonamplumbing.com.ng

Site Purpose

The site provides information about available plumbing products, services, and pricing. It serves as an online catalog, offers contact and inquiry forms for customers, and promotes new arrivals and promotions. It also serves contractors, plumbers, and homeowners looking for quality plumbing solutions.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable but durable modern taps for my home renovation?
  • How can I contact Jonam Plumbing Materials for a bulk purchase or quote?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#003366): Used for headings and navigation menu.
  • Sky Blue (#4da6ff): Used for backgrounds, buttons, and accent elements.

Typography

  • Segoe UI: Used for headings and general body text due to its clarity and professional appearance.
  • Roboto (optional): May be used for product descriptions and cards to ensure readability across devices.
